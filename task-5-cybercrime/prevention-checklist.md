# Stay Safe Online: 8-Point Prevention Checklist

1. **Financial Rule #1:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money. A PIN is only required when you are authorizing a payment to send funds to someone else.
2. **Financial Rule #2:** Always double-check the receiver's VPA (Virtual Payment Address) and the exact amount on your screen before authorizing any UPI payment.
3. **Password Security:** Use strong, unique passwords for your university portal and personal email, combining letters, numbers, and symbols.
4. **Account Protection:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all your primary accounts, especially your email, social media, and banking apps.
5. **Network Safety:** Never use free, public Wi-Fi at cafes, airports, or railway stations to make online purchases or check your bank balance. Use cellular data instead.
6. **Software Updates:** Keep your phone's operating system and payment apps updated to the latest versions to patch known security vulnerabilities.
7. **Phishing Awareness:** Be highly skeptical of any message (email, SMS, or WhatsApp) that creates a sense of extreme urgency, panic, or offers something that seems too good to be true.
8. **Official Reporting:** If you suspect you have been scammed, immediately freeze your bank account and report the incident to the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** or call the **Helpline 1930**.
